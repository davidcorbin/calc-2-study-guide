\section*{6.3 Logarithmic Functions}

The inverse of an exponential function is called a \textbf{logarithmic function} with base \(b\) and denoted by \( \log_b \).

\[ \log_b{x} = y \quad \Leftrightarrow \quad b^y = x \]
